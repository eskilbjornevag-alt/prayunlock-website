Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Pray Unlock ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Pray Unlock mobile application.

Information We Collect

Pray Unlock is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Name (optional): You may provide a first name during onboarding for personalized greetings. This is stored locally on your device only.
  • Prayer history: Your prayer completions, notes, mood check-ins, and streak data are stored locally on your device.
  • App blocking preferences: The list of apps you choose to block is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All your personal data ‚Äî including your name, prayer history, notes, and app preferences ‚Äî is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Pray Unlock may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play / Apple App Store: For app distribution. Their respective privacy policies apply to app downloads and payments.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions on Android:

  • Accessibility Service: Required to detect when a blocked app is opened and display the prayer overlay.
  • Display Over Other Apps: Required to show the prayer screen when a blocked app is launched.
  • Usage Access: Required to identify which apps are currently in use.

These permissions are used solely for the app-blocking feature and are never used to collect, transmit, or share your data.

Children's Privacy

Pray Unlock does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal data beyond what is stored locally on the device.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
